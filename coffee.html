COFFEE

  • Here's a general overview of how to prepare coffee using different methods, along with some common ingredients:
  • Coffee beans: Arabica or Robusta, depending on preference.
  • Water: Fresh, filtered water is ideal.
  • ingredients: Finely-ground espresso beans, water.
  • Add coarse-ground coffee to a filter, brew with water in a coffee maker.
  • Grind finely, fill portafilter, extract under pressure in an espresso machine.
  • Steps: Grind coffee, fill portafilter, lock in machine, extract under high pressure.
